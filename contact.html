Frequent Asked Question

How Do I return?

We’re working towards reducing the amount of plastic we use. When returning your items please put all items into one parcel bag and attach one returns label (regardless of the items coming back.) We’ll do the rest - once your parcel reaches us we’ll scan each item individually and credit them.

What is Our Refund Policy?

We are happy to refund or exchange any item within 28 days of purchase, provided the item is returned in a saleable condition with an original receipt. If you are returning unwanted items purchased via Click and Collect please return these items to any Click + Collect store.

How Can I Make a Payment?

We take any of these form of payment :

  • Credit Card Payment
  • Debit Card Payment
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cryptocurrency
